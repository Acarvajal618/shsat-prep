Distance, Rate, Time: Find Total Time

Rate = distance ÷ time. Then time = distance ÷ rate.

Strategy

The Approach

Use the known partial trip to find the rate (d÷t). Apply that rate to the full distance to find total time.

  • 🔍Partial distance and time given
  • 🔍"Same rate" or "constant speed"
  • 🔍Two-step: find rate first, then find total time

The Problem

A bus travels 1,070 miles. It covers the first 310 miles in 5 hours at a constant rate. How many total hours does the full trip take?

Step-by-Step Solution

    Find the rate.
    rate = 310 ÷ 5 = 62 mph
  2. 2
    Find total time.
    time = 1,070 ÷ 62 ≈ 17.3 hrs

Common Mistakes
