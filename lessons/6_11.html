Unit 6_11

Decimal to Percent Conversion

Move the decimal two places right to convert to percent.

Strategy

The Approach

Multiply the decimal by 100 to convert to a percentage. Moving the decimal point two places to the right is the same thing.

  • 🔍Multiply by 100: 0.0201 × 100 = 2.01%.
  • 🔍Look for the answer choice closest to 2.01%.
  • 🔍Watch for traps: 0.2% = 0.002 and 200% = 2.00.

The Problem

Which percentage is closest in value to 0.0201?

A0%
B0.2%
C2%✓
D200%

Step-by-Step Solution

  1. 1
    Convert decimal to percent.
    0.0201 × 100 = 2.01%
  2. 2
    Find the closest answer choice.
    2.01% is closest to C = 2%.
✅
Answer C — 2%
0.01 = 1%, 0.02 = 2%, 0.0201 ≈ 2% ✓

Common Mistakes

  • ❌
    Off by factor of 10B = 0.2% — that corresponds to 0.002, not 0.0201.
  • ❌
    Wrong directionD = 200% — that's 2.00 × 100, not 0.0201 × 100.
  • ✅
    Right moveMove decimal 2 places right: 0.0201 → 2.01%.
