Unit 6_2

Finding a Missing Value, Then Computing a Percentage

Find the missing row first, then percentage = part/whole × 100.

Strategy

Core Approach
When a table gives totals and most values, find the missing value by subtraction. Then compute the percentage: (missing value ÷ total) × 100.

Step-by-Step Solution

  • 1
    Find salami: 25 − 9 − 6 − 4 − 3 = 3 pounds of salami.
  • 2
    Percentage: 3/25 × 100 = 12%.
    Shortcut: 3/25 — multiply both by 4 → 12/100 = 12%. No calculator needed.
✅
Answer: D → 12%
3/25 = 12/100 = 12% ✓

Common Mistakes

  • ✗ Mistake Computing 3/25 and getting 0.12 — then forgetting to multiply by 100 to get %.
  • ✗ Mistake Using 3/22 (dividing by the other items, not the total).
