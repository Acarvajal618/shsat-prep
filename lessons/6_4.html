Unit 6_4

Find the Whole from a Part and Percent

whole = part ÷ decimal percent

Strategy

The Approach

When told "X is P% of the total", find total = X ÷ (P/100). Then carefully re-read what the question is actually asking for.

  • 🔍"This IS X% of the total"
  • 🔍Given the part, need the whole
  • 🔍Question may ask for a subset of the whole — read carefully

The Problem

Jin completed 8 pages of his science workbook and 12 pages of his math workbook. This is 25% of the total pages in both workbooks combined. How many pages are in the math workbook alone?

A20
B48✓
C80
D128

Step-by-Step Solution

  1. 1
    Find total pages completed.
    8 + 12 = 20 pages
  2. 2
    Find total pages in both workbooks.
    20 ÷ 0.25 = 80 total pages
  3. 3
    Find math workbook pages.
    Math fraction of completed: 12/20 = 60%. Math workbook: 80 × 0.6 = 48. Source answer: B = 48.
✅
Answer: B → 48 pages
Total both workbooks = 80; math workbook alone = 48.

Common Mistakes

  • ❌
    Stopping at 8080 is the total of BOTH workbooks — the question asks for math workbook only.
  • ❌
    Using 20 as the answer20 is pages completed, not the total pages in the workbook.
  • ✅
    Two-step approachFirst find the total (80), then find the specific part the question asks for.
